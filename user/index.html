Welcome to CCRP Dasboard

This is the dashboard of CCRP for those who have successfully registered and log into the system. You can file a new report of a crime and you can also track the progress of any other crime that you have filed.


To file a new case, you'll have to click on the Report Case link and fill all fields of the form. Please note that all fields are required.

You can also view all your cases that you have submitted and click on any case to view full details of the case.


You have the ability to track all your cases that you have reported. All the delegated law enforcement will be notified of your cases immediately you file a new case. You can then track the case and get feedback by clicking Track case.
