Welcome To CCRP Admin Dasboard

As an Administrator, you have the ability to view all cases submitted by a user and you can click on a particular case to view the details of that case.


You will also update the status of a case by clicking Update link and you can view all the cases as well as update the status of a case.


You also have the task of reporting any case to the appropriate authority to resolve the case. If a case is resolved, you have to update the status of the case. If a case is being processed, you will also update the status of the case to processing.
